Have an unidentified blemish on your skin that you're worried could be dangerous? Have limited access a dermatologist? Or maybe you're a doctor looking for a second opinion for their patient? Let DermaGraph be the solution to all these worries.

2 in 3 Australians develop some form of malignant skin cancer over their lifetime, and worldwide this figure is 1 in 50. While the mortality rate for skin cancer is relatively low, its side effects significantly hinder your quality of life. By providing accessible, on-demand diagnosis for skin lesions, DermaGraph will make sure that you or your patient will not be a part of these statistics.

How do we do this? DermaGraph utilises an industry-standard convolutional neural network (CNN) that uses a picture of your lesion, along with your demographics, to determine the likelihood of it being malignant with 91% accuracy. Meanwhile, dermatologists only have an 86.6% accuracy with the naked eye. Thus, DermaGraph has several benefits:

  • Studies show that when detected early, the 5-year survival rate for melanoma is 99%.
  • DermaGraph operates solely through photographs, meaning you will not have to undergo an invasive biopsy.
  • In most cases, the chance of malignancy is so low that you do not need to visit a dermatologist. While it is always better to do so, we acknowledge that there are many who have limited access to dermatologists.
